What Is Leh We Fen Wok?

Leh We Fen Wok is a youth focused platform created to help young people in Sierra Leone access opportunities more easily.

Many opportunities are shared across different WhatsApp groups, Facebook pages, and websites, making them difficult to find. This platform helps bring everything together in one place.

Our goal is to support young people in Sierra Leone with access to jobs, internships, skills training, and career information.
